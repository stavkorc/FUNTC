FunTime Commands


/piona /aqua /fonix /focus1 /honey /melix /hardy - for one of these promo codes, you will be given a free case.

/vk - you can link your account to the VKontakte page.

/ah - open an auction /ah search (name of any item in Russian)

/design - you can change the design (addresses, nickname color, etc.)

/shop - here you can buy something for tokens, as well as buy emeralds for coins

/spawn - teleport to spawn

/warp mine, /warp pvp - /warp mine teleports you to the auto-mine, /warp pvp teleports you to the pvp arena.

/buyer - open the buyer

/salary - get salary (you get coins for every hour you play)

/kit get/buy Kit-Set

/m (player nickname) Send a private message to the player

/r Reply to the last player you wrote to

/hub - teleport to the hub (I don't recommend going there in PvP mode)

/clan create - create a clan

/clan help - find out all the commands for the clan.

/rtp - teleport to any point in the world

/event delay - how much time is left until the event.
